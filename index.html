The Resource Pack

Voidpack is a combination pack of textures from CyanideX's Unity, Jappa's 1.14.x Textures (backported by myself), and others. A few textures are even made by me, but by no means do I take credit for every texture in the pack! This pack is a 16x16 pack which blends in seamlessly with any modpack in Minecraft 1.12.

Downloads

This pack is kept up-to-date using GitHub, as it's versioning support and downloadability is perfect. The resource pack is kept in downloadable format on the repo, so if you click "Download Repository as zip", you can place that zip directly into your resource packs directory.
